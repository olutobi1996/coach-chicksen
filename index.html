Why Choose Us?

With over 15 years of top-level professional football experience—including time in the English Championship and international appearances for Zimbabwe—Adam Chicksen brings a wealth of expertise, passion, and professionalism to youth coaching.

  • Elite Experience: Played professionally from age 16, representing clubs like MK Dons, Brighton & Hove Albion, and Notts County.
  • International Perspective: Featured for the Zimbabwe national team, bringing global football knowledge into grassroots coaching.
  • Youth-Centric Coaching: Focused on helping 3–8 year olds develop through structured play, fun drills, and confidence-building games.
  • Inclusive Role Model: A multicultural background and leadership mindset create a welcoming, respectful, and team-first environment.

Adam is more than just a coach—he's a mentor who inspires young players to fall in love with football while developing key life skills through the joy of sport.
